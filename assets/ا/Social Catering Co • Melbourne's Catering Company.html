Our Latest Events

Melbourne City Chiro

Business Anniversary
Catering for 40 people

The team at Melbourne City Chiro wanted to mark 10 years in business with a big celebration. The event catered for 40 people including their employees and clients. Held at their city offices, it was a cocktail-style function with a mixed menu of delicious finger foods paired with carefully selected wines.

Charge CrossFit

Health and fitness retreat
Catering for 15 people

Charge CrossFit in Northcote hold regular retreats focussed on healthy eating, fitness, weightlifting technique, goal setting, mindset and meditation. The menus are hearty, to fuel participants’ active days, as well as organic, healthy and tasty. As part of the retreats, Jade performs a cooking demonstration and leaves participants with a few healthy meal ideas and recipes they can integrate into their lifestyles.

NAB Mini Legends

Photo shoot
Catering for 25 people

NAB Mini legends is a campaign promoting NAB’s support of grassroots football in Australia, through its partnership with Auskick. Working with Photographer, Christopher Tovo, the stills shoot catered for adults as well as kids for breakfast, lunch and afternoon tea.
